GPA Calculator

How to use

Enter your scores in the format, 5,4,3,2,1,4,3,2,1 where each number corresponds to a grade as follows:

  • A=5
  • B=4
  • C=3
  • D=2
  • E=1
and units in the respective fields.







Screening Calculator

How to use

Enter your JAMB score and O level score in the respective fields. The screening score is calculated as follows:

  • Screening Score = [(JAMB Score / 400) * 60 + (sum of O Level Score / 30) * 40]
  • Where O Level Score is the average of your best 5 subjects.
  • Ensure that your JAMB score is out of 400 and O Level score is out of 30.
    • Note:your O'level grades are expected to be in the format,
    • A1=6
    • B2=5
    • B3=4
    • C4=3
    • C5=2
    • C6=1
